Forest Fighters

Made by Henry Dang and Edan Sneh at Gunn Hacks 4.0

Use WASD to control P1 E = Upercut, R = Punch, F = Low Kick.
Use the arrow keys to control P2 N = Uppercut, M = Punch, ',' = Low Kick.
Choose your attacks wisely, if you or your opponent choose the same
attack they will cancel each other out. Uppercuts defeat jumps and
crouching defeats the low kick.
